I'm excited to share **Mya View** - a personal health companion that puts privacy first.

**The Problem:**

Healthcare is overwhelming. Between juggling doctor appointments, medications, and medical history for multiple family members, it's easy to forget critical details. Most health apps either charge subscription fees, sell your data to advertisers, or require uploading sensitive information to the cloud.

**The Solution:**

Mya View is an open-source health companion that runs 100% locally on your device. Your health data never leaves your computer. No cloud services. No data collection. No subscriptions.
